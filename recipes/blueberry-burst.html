Blueberry Burst

Description

This blueberry potion balances sweetness and tartness perfectly.

Ingredients

  • 6 oz blueberries, fresh NOT frozen
  • 5 oz cranberry juice
  • 5 oz plain yogurt
  • (optional) honey, to taste

Steps

  1. Combine blueberries and cranberry juice in blender, process 2 minutes for maximum smooth.
  2. Add yogurt and blend briefly to combine.
  3. Taste and add honey for extra sweetness, blend to combine.
  4. Pour into a chilled glass, add straws if desired.
