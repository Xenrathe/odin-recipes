Almond & Banana Smoothie

Description

This potent concoction of strong flavors is sure to satisfy even the most jaded imbibers.

Ingredients

  • 4.5 oz blanched almonds (buy or google how to blanch almonds - it's fun!)
  • 1 pint soy, almond, or oat milk
  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ground cinnamon for sprinkling

Steps

  1. Process blanched almonds in blender until finely chopped.
  2. Add milk, bananas, and vanilla extract and blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into glass and sprinkle top with cinnamon.
