Pear & Raspberry Breeze

Description

A combination of two delicate fruit flavors creates a light, refreshing drink.

Ingredients

  • 1 large ripe pear, of any type
  • 4.5 oz raspberries, frozen or fresh
  • 7 oz cold water

Steps

  1. Peel and quarter pear, removing the core.
  2. Put pear quarters, raspberries, and cold water in blender, process until smooth.
  3. Pour into glasses and decorate with whole raspberries on cocktail stick, if desired.
