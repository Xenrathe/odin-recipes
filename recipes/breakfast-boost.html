Breakfast Boost

Description

This blend of citrus is great for humans! But not for dogs, who cannot have citrus (sorry Atlas!).

Ingredients

  • 14 oz canned grapefruit and orange segments
  • 4 tbsp lemon juice
  • 3 tbsp lime juice
  • 15 oz orange juice

Steps

  1. Pour contents of can (fruit + can juices) into blender.
  2. Add the lemon, lime, and orange juices into blender, process until smooth.
  3. Pour into chilled glasses and enjoy.
