Kiwi Shake

Description

This kiwi shake will shake your flavor buds so much it'll register on the Richter scale.

Ingredients

  • 5 oz dairy milk
  • 4 tbsp lime juice (2 limes worth)
  • 2 kiwi fruit, chopped
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 14 oz vanilla ice cream

Steps

  1. Pour milk and lime juice into blender and process gently/briefly, just to combine.
  2. Add the kiwi fruit and sugar and process gently, just to combine.
  3. Add the ice cream and process until smooth.
  4. Pour mixture into glasses and decorate with kiwi slices and/or lime peel.
  5. Drink right away (does not stay good long).
