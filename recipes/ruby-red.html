Ruby Smoothsday

Description

The inimitable flavor of grapefruit comes through strongly in this tasty beverage.

Ingredients

  • 1 large pink or ruby grapefruit
  • 3.5 oz cold water
  • 3.5 oz Greek yogurt
  • 1 tbsp honey

Steps

  1. Quarter the grapefruit, then pull off the peel and as much pith as possible. Discard seeds.
  2. Put the grapefruit and water into blender and process until smooth.
  3. Add the yogurt and honey and process until combined.
  4. Pour into glasses and decorate with slice of grapefruit if desired.
