Orange & Strawberry Cream

Description

A delicious orange and strawberry smoothie. A classic!

Ingredients

  • 4 oz plain yogurt
  • 6 oz strawberry yogurt
  • 6 oz orange juice (recommend pulp free)
  • 6 oz strawberries, frozen or fresh
  • 1 banana, frozen or fresh

Steps

  1. Combine yogurts in blender and blend gently.
  2. Add orange juice and blend.
  3. Add strawberries and banana and blend until smooth.
  4. Pour into glass and decorate with banana and/or strawberry slices.
